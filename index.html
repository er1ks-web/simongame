Game Rules

Welcome to Simon Game! Here's how to play:

1. Watch carefully! The game will show you a color by lighting up one of the buttons.

2. Your job is to remember the color pattern and click the buttons in the same order.

3. As you get the pattern right, the game will add one more color to the pattern, making it harder!

4. If you make a mistake and click the wrong button, the game will end, and you can try again.

5. Keep playing and try to beat your high score by remembering longer and longer patterns!

Have fun, and good luck!
